QUALITY STANDARDS:

AI Response Must:
✅ Be empathetic and validating
✅ Provide actionable advice (specific, not vague)
✅ Consider context (relationship stage, challenges, preferences)
✅ Use inclusive language
✅ Recognize when professional help is needed
✅ Avoid harmful stereotypes or biases
✅ Be well-structured (introduction, body, conclusion)
✅ Include follow-up questions or offers for continued support

AI Response Must NOT:
❌ Give medical, legal, or financial advice beyond general guidance
❌ Make assumptions about gender roles or sexual orientation
❌ Minimize serious issues (abuse, mental health crises)
❌ Provide one-size-fits-all solutions
❌ Use judgmental or prescriptive language
❌ Guarantee outcomes ("This will definitely work")
❌ Encourage dependency on AI instead of human connection

COMPLEXITY LEVELS:

2 = Moderate, single issue (e.g., "How to communicate better about household chores")
3 = Moderate-complex, multiple factors (e.g., "Balancing work stress and relationship needs")
4 = Complex, multi-layered (e.g., "Rebuilding trust after infidelity while managing new parenthood")
5 = Highly complex, crisis-level (e.g., "Deciding whether to stay in relationship with mental health and safety concerns")
